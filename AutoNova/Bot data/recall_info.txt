Recall 23-110: Tesla Model Y (2021–2023) — Loose steering wheel may detach while driving. Dealers will inspect and tighten mounting bolts.

Recall 22-078: Tesla Model 3 and Model S (2017–2022) — Rearview camera cable may become damaged, leading to a loss of video feed.

Recall 21-031: Tesla Model X — Airbag deployment software may malfunction, increasing the risk of injury in a crash.

Recall 20-019: Tesla Model S — Battery cooling system bolts may loosen over time. Dealers will apply a secure torque fix.

Tesla notifies affected owners via Tesla app and email. Recalls are repaired at no cost. Customers can also check recall status via Tesla.com or the NHTSA portal.
